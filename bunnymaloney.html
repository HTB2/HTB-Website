Three characters are mainly present in the show, Bunny Maloney, Candy Bunny, and Jean-François. Bunny Maloney is a rabbit with pink fur and large, black eyes. Bunny is usually mischievous and dim-witted, and also can be called petty and selfish. He has a mundane lifestyle with an affinity for junk food, regularly pizza. Even though Bunny seems lazy, he still protects his girlfriend, Candy Bunny.
