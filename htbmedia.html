About HTBMedia

HTB Media, comprised of HTB TV, Zamination, GameToons, BlipToons, AvusWavus, HC Cartoons and several syndication partnerships, is a leading online entertainment company targeting a mature and core audience of teenagers and young adults age 15-34.

Founded in 2024 by yours truly Harry J. Coffee, HTB Media delivers daily comedic content, through videos, pictures and jokes, created and/or curated by the staff. HTB Media attracts more than 20 million fans yearly and generates more than 20 million video views per month with popular shows such as “Eddsworld Fan Eddisode,” and “Beendows X” HTB Media provides sketches across a variety of categories with an efficient vehicle for reaching the desirable young adult and teenager demographic.

The HTB TV brand extends offline with books, television programs, and a sketch.
