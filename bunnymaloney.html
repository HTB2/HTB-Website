2009
Amidst the neon signs and the pulsing rhythms of Bunnyville, you will find four very unlikely friends : Bunny Maloney, Candy Bunny, Jean-François and Stan Ookie. At their worst, they are a conflicted band of self-absorbed neurotics. At their best, they are the self-appointed Superhero Crime Fighters of Protecteam who combat the daily diabolical threats of the evil scientist Professor Débilouman.
